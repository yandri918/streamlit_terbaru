Professional Background

I'm Andriyanto, BScE, S.E, an AI Product Engineer specializing in agricultural technology solutions. With dual degrees in Computer Engineering and Economics, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to solve real-world agricultural challenges.

My journey in AgriTech began with a simple question: "How can we use AI to help Indonesian farmers increase productivity and profitability?" This led to the creation of the AgriSensa ecosystem – a comprehensive platform serving thousands of farmers across Indonesia.

Currently based in Japan, I combine international best practices with local Indonesian agricultural knowledge to create solutions that are both technologically advanced and culturally relevant.
